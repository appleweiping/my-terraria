## Usage Notes

- This world file requires the **tsorcRevamp** tModLoader mod to function as intended
- Without the mod, the world loads in vanilla Terraria but custom enemies, items, Dark Souls mechanics, and progression will be absent
- Designed for tModLoader 1.4.4 + Terraria 1.4.4.9
- Supports 1-4 players
